Handling "Our End" Minting & Issuance

The contracts in this repository (troptions-ucc) are attestation and governance tools only. They do not contain mint, burn, or issuance logic.

Correct Flow
  1. Legal pledge executed and perfected (UCC-1 filing by Troptions as authorized secured party).
  2. Document hashes registered on-chain via DocumentHashRegistry (Apostle first).
  3. Reserve status attested via TroptionsReserveRegistry (700M USD cash + Scotia custody reference).
  4. The on-chain attested reserve status becomes an input to Troptions capital/issuance policy engines on the UnyKorn/Apostle sovereign control plane.
  5. Any actual token issuance or "minting" (e.g. related to TROPTIONS capital activities, USDF, or other instruments) is handled in the broader Troptions issuance systems, gated by the attested collateral position.

The 700M cash remains at Scotia Bank Canada. The pledge creates a perfected security interest. The on-chain registries provide the auditable, event-sourced proof for governance, due diligence, and internal capital decisions.
